About Us

Why Moon Coffee Cafe?



Moon Coffee Cafe, is India’s favourite hangout for coffee and conversations. We strive to provide the best experience to our guests.Our coffees are sourced from thousands of small coffee planters, who made us who we are today and we're glad to be a part of their lives.

Our Coffee – We serve a range of espresso coffees made from the finest coffee beans, roasted right here in Cape Coral, and our baristas really know their beans. With their superior technical knowledge and a passion for making amazing coffee they will make you the perfect cuppa.

We hope our cafe will become part of your neighborhood and lifestyle.
